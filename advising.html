Advising Resources

Academic Advising at UMSI

UMSI advisors are here to help you navigate your graduate experience. Whether you're deciding on a specialization, planning your schedule, exploring career options, or needing support, our advising resources can help guide you every step of the way.

General Advising

Meet with academic advisors to plan your semester, discuss degree requirements, and explore specializations. Available in person or virtually by appointment.

Career Counseling

Work one-on-one with career advisors to develop your career goals, improve your resume and cover letter, prepare for interviews, and explore internships or job opportunities.

Workshops and Info Sessions

Attend events on topics like course registration, resume writing, career exploration, internship search, and graduate school prep. Check your email or advising calendar regularly.

Peer Advising

Connect with trained UMSI student peer advisors who offer advice on classes, student life, and navigating your first year. Peer advisors hold office hours throughout the week.

Advising Checklist

  • ✅ Check your academic progress in Wolverine Access
  • ✅ Prepare questions about courses, specializations, or requirements
  • ✅ Bring your unofficial transcript or have it pulled up digitally
  • ✅ Know your upcoming deadlines (add/drop, registration, etc.)
  • ✅ Think about your career and long-term goals

When to Contact an Advisor

  • Need help choosing classes? → General Advising
  • Confused about your degree audit? → General Advising
  • Looking for an internship? → Career Counseling
  • Want to switch your specialization? → General or Peer Advising
  • Feeling overwhelmed or uncertain? → Any advisor — we're here for you

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I schedule an advising appointment? Use the Advising Scheduler or email advising@umich.edu.
  • How often should I meet with an advisor? We recommend at least once per semester to stay on track.
  • Are advising meetings available virtually? Yes! You can choose between in-person or Zoom appointments.
  • Can I talk to more than one type of advisor? Absolutely — you can meet with general, peer, or career advisors depending on your need.

Plan Ahead and Stay Connected

Advising appointments fill up quickly during registration season. Schedule early and stay up to date with UMSI advising newsletters and announcements.

Visit the UMSI Advising Portal for appointment links, forms, and more resources.
