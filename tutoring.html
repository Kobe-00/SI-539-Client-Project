Tutoring Resources

Find the Right Tutoring Support

Explore various tutoring options available at UMSI to help you succeed academically. Whether you need help understanding a concept, revising a paper, or reviewing for an exam, there's a support service for you.

Writing Center

Get help with writing assignments, thesis projects, and research papers. The Writing Center offers personalized feedback on structure, clarity, grammar, and citations—whether you’re just starting a draft or polishing your final version.

Math & Stats Tutoring

Struggling with data analysis, algorithms, or statistical concepts? Connect with tutors who specialize in topics like data science, regression modeling, and probability. One-on-one and drop-in sessions available.

Peer Tutoring Programs

Join weekly study groups or schedule a peer-led tutoring session with students who have taken the same courses. Peer tutors understand the course expectations and can provide practical tips from experience.

TA Office Hours

Each course typically offers scheduled office hours with Teaching Assistants (TAs). These sessions are perfect for asking clarifying questions, getting feedback on assignments, or reviewing for exams in a relaxed setting.

How to Access Tutoring

  • Writing Center: Book an appointment online via Sweetland
  • Math/Stats Support: View schedules and sign up
  • Peer Tutoring: Reach out through UMSI’s academic support system or course Discord/Canvas
  • TA Hours: Check your course syllabus or Canvas announcements

Tips for Academic Success

  • ✅ Don’t wait until the last minute—seek help early and regularly
  • ✅ Bring specific questions or a draft to tutoring sessions
  • ✅ Attend office hours weekly to build rapport with instructors
  • ✅ Form or join study groups with classmates
  • ✅ Make use of planners or task apps to manage deadlines

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are tutoring services free? Yes, all listed services are free for U-M students.
  • Do I need an appointment? Writing Center and some math sessions require appointments; others are drop-in.
  • Can I use multiple tutoring services at once? Absolutely! Combine them based on your needs.
  • Can international students use tutoring services? Yes, all are welcome. Writing tutors are also trained in ESL support.
